Modular Add-ons

Showduino supports external props and modules known as add-ons. These range from lighting props like haunted lanterns to puzzle devices like the R3 Terminal.

Industrial escape room prop featuring 3 buttons, a magnetic lock, and LED feedback. Effects include:

  • • Console Flicker

  • • Smoke Control

Timeline FX Support

Add-ons integrate with the GoreFX Timeline Editor, letting you drag and drop FX events onto prop-specific tracks with full control over timing, playback, and intensity.
