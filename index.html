Touchscreen UI

Experience the future of show control with Showduino's intuitive touchscreen user interface. Navigate effortlessly through your lighting and FX settings with just a fingertip. Our responsive design ensures that you can make quick adjustments on the fly, allowing for real-time changes during performances. Whether you're in the heat of a haunted house show or orchestrating a chilling ambiance for a scare, the touchscreen UI puts complete control at your fingertips. Enjoy a user-friendly experience that simplifies complex operations and enhances your creative expression in any haunted attraction.

NeoPixel FX Control

With Showduino’s NeoPixel FX Control, you can manage multiple lighting zones, customize animations, and control brightness in real time. Our advanced system supports addressable LEDs with a stunning range of effects – from slow eerie glows to wild strobe bursts. Whether you're creating a moody dungeon or a chaotic chase scene, NeoPixel FX gives you theatrical control at your fingertips.

Diagnostics + Self-Test

When you’re running a haunted attraction, failure is not an option — unless it’s a fake-out scare. That’s where Showduino’s Diagnostics + Self-Test feature steps in. This powerful tool ensures your system is performing at its best before guests even step into the scream zone.

From checking relay statuses to verifying NeoPixel responses, the Diagnostics engine runs a full sweep of your system hardware and communication lines. You'll know in seconds if something’s offline, out of sync, or behaving badly. With manual test modes and automated self-checks, it’s like having a ghostbuster in your control panel.

Whether you're troubleshooting a flickering LED or stress-testing a full haunt setup, this menu gives you detailed status reports and confidence that everything is show-ready. Think of it as your pre-scare checklist — complete with voltage readings, temperature sensors, I/O health, and relay integrity.

Infrared Remote Integration

Sometimes, old-school tech brings the most reliable scares. Showduino supports Infrared (IR) remote control, allowing you to access menus, trigger FX, and run diagnostics with a simple click — no WiFi, no wires, just instant spooky action.

Whether you're hiding backstage or need to operate the system from a shadowy control booth, IR gives you handheld control that’s fast, discreet, and effective. Perfect for traditionalists, backup systems, or those who just love pointing a remote and feeling like a haunt overlord.

Assign custom commands to your remote buttons, use it as a hidden control panel, or integrate it into your actor-triggered scares. With Showduino’s IR support, you’ve got an invisible hand controlling the terror.
