Bluetooth & WiFi

Cut the Cords – Embrace the Signal

Overview

Showduino connects wirelessly to your haunt tech with seamless Bluetooth and WiFi integration. Whether you're syncing shows or running diagnostics, you’re always in control – even from the shadows.

Wireless Capabilities

  • • Real-time WiFi scanning with signal strength indicators
  • • Long-press to forget networks (popup confirmation)
  • • Infrared remote input for WiFi passwords
  • • Bluetooth toggling via touchscreen or remote trigger

HauntSync Integration

Enable live diagnostics and scene control from your browser with HauntSync – Showduino’s web-based command portal. Use it to coordinate team effects or trigger props remotely across the network.
